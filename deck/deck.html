OXAR · How it Works08 / 15
How it Works

Underlying
magic.

Every dollar of TVL is backed 1:1 by an off-chain custodian holding the real bond. The protocol just makes the wrapper composable, transferable, and instant-settlement.

Core

PDA Vaults

One Solana program, deterministic vaults per bond series. Audited bytecode, no admin upgrades.

Yield

Daily NAV Accrual

NAV ticks every slot. No rebases, no claim transactions until maturity.

Settlement

USDC In, USDC Out

Off-chain custody mints the bond, on-chain settlement uses Circle USDC for liquidity.

Compliance

TransferHook + KYC

Token-2022 hook enforces 40-day Reg S lockup and allowlist for U.S. persons.
