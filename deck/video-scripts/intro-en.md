---

## Delivery notes

- **"We removed all three"** — pause 1–1.5 sec after the line. Let the words land. This is the strongest moment of the hook.
- **"The yield always existed. The access never did."** — say the second sentence slower, with falling intonation on "never did".
- **"Hold it. Trade it. Send it."** — short hits, with micro-pauses between words. Interface language, not description.
- **"First market: Ukraine. Next: yours."** — calmest. No exclamation. Closes with conviction, not a sales push.

---

## What was deliberately left out of the video

- Compliance stack (Token-2022 TransferHook, Reg S, KYC) — engineering trust signal, not for a 60-second intro
- NAV mechanics in detail — replaced with "the token earns by itself"
- Diaspora focus — universal positioning without ethnic anchoring
- Bonds-vs-stocks insight — kept for Twitter content, not for video
