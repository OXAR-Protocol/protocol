# OXAR — Brand Guidelines

## Name

- Full: **OXAR Protocol**
- Short: **OXAR**
- Always uppercase: OXAR, never Oxar or oxar
- Pronunciation: "OX-ar" (rhymes with "Oscar" without the c)

## Tagline

**Emerging market yields, onchain.**

## Alternative Copy Lines

- "Ondo gave crypto 4%. OXAR gives 16%."
- "Government bonds. Global yields. One click."
- "The yield layer for emerging markets."
- "Real yields. Real bonds. Real simple."

## Token Naming Convention

Vault tokens follow the pattern: `ox` + country code + `-` + currency

| Token | Full Name | Vault |
|-------|-----------|-------|
| oxUA-UAH | OXAR Ukraine Hryvnia Bond | Ukrainian OVDP (UAH) |
| oxUA-USD | OXAR Ukraine Dollar Bond | Ukrainian OVDP (USD) |
| oxUS-TBILL | OXAR US Treasury Bill | US T-Bills |
| oxTR-TRY | OXAR Turkey Lira Bond | Turkish govt bonds |
| oxPL-PLN | OXAR Poland Zloty Bond | Polish treasury bonds |
| oxBR-BRL | OXAR Brazil Real Bond | Brazilian sovereign debt |

## Voice & Tone

- **Clear over clever** — we deal with real money, be direct
- **Confident but honest** — show yields AND risks transparently
- **Global** — speak to investors worldwide, not just one region
- **Not hype-driven** — no "to the moon", no "guaranteed returns"

## Color Palette (suggested)

- Primary: Deep blue (#1A1F36) — trust, finance
- Accent: Electric teal (#00D4AA) — crypto-native, fresh
- Warning: Amber (#F5A623) — for risk indicators
- Background: Clean white/dark variants

## Design Principles

1. **Data-first** — show numbers, yields, charts prominently
2. **Comparison-driven** — always contextualize (vs Ondo, vs bank deposit)
3. **Risk-transparent** — never hide FX risk or sovereign risk
4. **Clean and minimal** — Ondo-level polish, not DeFi-degen aesthetic
